Chameleon, Master of Disguise



A chameleon's skin, Mirrors nature's artistry, Ever-changing hue.

Scientific Name: Chamaeleonidae
Average Length: 14-18 inches
Diet: Insects
Habitat: Forests

A cunning and versatile warrior, Chameleon can blend into any environment and strike with deadly precision. Its prehensile tail allows for swift movement, and its projectile tongue can snatch prey from afar. Beware of its tactical mind and cunning strategy, for Chameleon is a true master of the art of deception!
